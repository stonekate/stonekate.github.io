About me

March 18, 2025

I am a psycholinguist in the Department of Psychology at the University of Hull, UK. My research interests are in probabilistic sentence processing and how uncertainty and conflict affect readers' linguistic expectations. I use EEG, eye tracking, and other methods to examine how these expectations unfold over time.
